Happy Birthday & Mother's Day

Born on the fifth, beneath a soft spring sky,
A grace resides in you that time cannot defy.
With every passing year, your beauty grows profound,
A steady, quiet strength where love and truth are found.

You hold our home with hands so firm and kind,
A rarer, deeper soul I know I’ll never find.
You teach our little ones the path that’s right and true,
I see the face of God reflected back in you.

From birthday candles lit to honor your birth date,
To Mother’s Day, where we celebrate your noble state.
The way you nurture, lead, and guard our sacred space,
Proves you walk in wisdom, dignity, and grace.

My partner, my heart, my anchor in the fray,
I celebrate your life and spirit every single day.
Happy Birthday, and Happy Mother’s Day,
To the one who lights my world in every single way.
