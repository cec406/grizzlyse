Why Choose Grizzly for Your Network & Internet?

We design, install, and configure robust networks tailored to your location and needs. Whether you're dealing with rural dead zones, spotty traditional broadband, or need fast, stable WiFi/ethernet for work or home, we get you connected grizzly-tough: secure, efficient, and built to last in Montana's challenging environments.

  • Site survey and planning for optimal coverage and performance
  • Starlink dish installation, mounting, and router integration for high-speed satellite internet
  • Wired Ethernet runs (Cat6/Cat6a) for reliable, low-latency connections
  • Wireless access point setup (mesh systems, extenders) for whole-home or office coverage
  • Router/firewall configuration with security best practices
  • Troubleshooting existing setups and optimization for speed/stability

Perfect for: remote work from cabins or ranches, small business offices, smart home devices, video streaming, or any setup needing dependable internet in hard-to-reach Montana spots.
